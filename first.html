About Share A Bite

Share A Bite is a platform that bridges the gap between food donors, NGOs, and the hungry. Our mission is to reduce food waste and ensure no person or animal sleeps hungry. Together, we can build a hunger-free world.

How It Works

1. Donate

Food donors register and upload extra food details.

2. Connect

NGOs and individuals nearby are notified in real-time.

3. Deliver

Volunteers or partners pick up and distribute the food.

4. Track

Donors and receivers get delivery status and feedback.
